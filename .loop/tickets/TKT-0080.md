- Historical `Step*`, Japanese numbered work-round references, `Completed preparation steps`, and informal `operational note` wording have been removed or rewritten in the target files when they describe past migration chronology instead of current canonical behavior.
- Historical `OPS-*` wording remains only where it is required as a formal ticket ID, gate ID, schema field, evidence ID, command/report identifier, or current policy prerequisite.
- Any remaining `Step*` or `OPS-*` match is recorded in a machine-readable report with a reason explaining why it is still canonical and must remain.
- The required `rg` commands are run and their outputs are summarized in the ticket evidence.
- The Phase1 gameplay start packet is regenerated after the cleanup passes, and `TKT-0005` start eligibility is re-evaluated from the generated evidence.
- No user-selected songs, charts, audio, images, videos, private links, `.env` files, OAuth credentials, service-account JSON, `OPENAI_API_KEY`, `CODEX_API_KEY`, `openai/codex-action@v1`, or `pull_request_target` are introduced.
